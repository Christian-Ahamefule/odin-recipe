Egg Sauce Recipe

Description

A plate of mouth watering egg sauce. One of the best choice for African family gatherings and a sure means to test your ability to prepare that lovely dish you see on TV. Best combination for yam.

Meal prep just got easy!

Ingredients

  • 6 raw eggs
  • 6 or 4 seeds of small or big fresh tomato respectively
  • Groundnut oil
  • water
  • 1 onion
  • Fresh pepper
  • salt

Steps

  1. Slice your tomatoes, onions, and pepper
  2. Break eggs and whisk together with sliced spices
  3. Add little quantity of water to mixture
  4. Heat oil in thick-bottom pan
  5. Add the mixture to oil and fry for 3 minutes
  6. Serve sauce with rice or yam!
