Chicken Recipe

Description

A plate of mouth watering well spiced chicken. The best choice for family gathering and a sure means to test your ability to prepare that lovely dish you see on TV.

Meal prep just got easy!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3 1/2 pounds bone-in, skin-on chicken parts

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken in buttermilk mixture
  2. Dredge chicken
  3. Heat oil in thick-bottom pan
  4. Mild-fry the chicken
  5. Remove to a crack to drain excess oil
  6. Coat with hot tomato sauce
